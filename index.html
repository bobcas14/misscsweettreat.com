Meet the Baker

Cassandra Hill

Welcome to Miss C's Sweet Treats! I'm Cassandra Hill, the heart and hands behind every cake, cupcake, and sweet creation you see here. Baking has always been my passion — a way to bring joy, celebrate milestones, and sweeten life's most precious moments.

From birthdays and weddings to holidays, funerals, and everything in between, I pour love into every recipe. Whether it's a towering custom cake or a box of assorted sweet treats, my goal is always the same: to make your occasion unforgettable.

"Homemade is where the heart is" — Every sweet treat is made from scratch, with premium ingredients and a whole lot of love.
